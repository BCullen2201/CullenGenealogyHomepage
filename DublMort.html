Cullen: Recorded Deaths
in Co Dublin
from the research notes of Gar Cullen
Gar has many years of research into his Cullen Genealogy and has been very gracious in sharing his notes with us. His specific interest is currently the ancestry of Ambrose Cullen, son of James Cullen a stone mason. Ambrose was born abt 1823 and married Ann Jane Niblock 19 Dec 1850 in Belfast. A son James was born 11 April 1852. Ambrose married again to Jane Tracey and had a daughter Jane born 16 Jan 1854. There is a suspicion that James the stone mason may have been from Co Wicklow. The names Cullen and Tracey are both common there. You can read the full query on the Co Wicklow posting page. If anyone is able to help with this family, please contact either Gar or myself.

List of sources for the research notes:
  • Banns, early marriages recorded in a variety of books in National Library and National Archives.
  • Index to wills in the National Archives. Selected wills in the National Archives.
  • Gravestone information in both the National Archives and Public Records Office, Belfast.
  • Church records (generally Co Wicklow and Co Leitrim and Belfast) in the National Library, Dublin and Public Records Office, Belfast.
  • Civil in Registry Office, Dublin and Church of LDS, Holywood Rd, Belfast and Altringham Rd, Manchester.
  • Church records in Church of Ireland "Representive Church Body Library" in Rathgar.
  • Street records in the directories in Dublin National Library and Belfast Public Records Office.
  • Some newspapers in Trinity College and Belfast Linenhall Library IGI.
