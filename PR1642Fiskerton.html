Jim Cullen

This is a transcription of the Protestation Return for Fiskerton, Nottinghamshire. It was not dated but was probably taken in early March of 1641 (1642 on the new calendar).


Protestation Return for Fiskerton, Nottinghamshire: March of 1641/42


​Heare is all the names and sir names of all the in habitance in Fiskerton

John Maifeeld
Edward Parker

Josife Blank

William Swift - Constable
Richard Devill - church warden and overseer of the pore
hath taken the ('oath of' crossed out) there protestation before the justises of pease.

These are to certify [th]at the inhabitants of Fiskerton whole names are abovewritten have taken the protestation in presence of us.

Francis Withington - Vicar
Richard Devell - Church Warden
William Swift - Constable

Fiskerton Finis
