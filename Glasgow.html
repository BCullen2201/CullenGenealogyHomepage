Register of Testaments - Scotland

Glasgow Commissariot 1800-1824

  • Cullen, William, Vitriol-Maker, near Govan, Coalwork, afterwards Napiershall near Blackquarry, Glasgow. February 1, 1820. Volume 80, Folio 625.

Edinburgh Commissariot
  • Cullen, Jess, Edinburgh. June 6, 1812. Volume 138 Folio 473.

  • Cullen, Mary Russell or, Alexander, St. Lucia. January 6, 1819. Volume 145, Folio 3. * See Note below.

  • Cullen, Hon. Robert, Senator of the College of Justice (Lord Cullen). July 17, 1811. Vol. 138, Folio 280.

Wigtown Commissariot - 1801-1824
  • Cullen, Alexander, shoemaker in Whithorn. May 31, 1806. Vol. W, Folio 63. Testament.

Stirling Commissariot - 1801-1824
  • Cullens, James, residenter in Stirling. October 22, 1804. Vol. W, Folio 57. Testament.

Dunbarton Commissariot - 1801-1824
  • Cullens, Robert, Feuar in Doune. January 23, 1808 Vol. W, Folio 86 Testament.

* Note: The entry for Cullen, Mary Russell in St. Lucia indicates where she died but that the testament was proved in Edinburgh. Also, the dates given in the above testaments are not the date of death but the date that the testament or inventory was recorded. This could sometimes be years after the person died.
